# Chapter 7: Humans Rising

**Target Word Count**: 1,000-1,500 words
**Time Period**: ~300,000 years ago to peak of civilization
**Mode of Competition**: Representation — what models reality best, wins

## Writing Notes

### Core Description (~400-600 words)
- What they were: Symbolic-thinking, tool-making, culture-building primates
- How they operated: Abstraction, modeling futures, technology, language, art
- What they couldn't conceive: Architecture of thought itself being surpassed
- **CRITICAL**: Do NOT use the word "human" or "humanity" until Chapter 9 reveal

### Transition Preview (~200-300 words)
- How they will be superseded: By machine minds (preview, not full reveal)
- Introduction of new dimension: Architecture of thought (hint only)
- Pattern reinforcement: Greatest strength becomes greatest vulnerability

### Narrator's Reflection (~400-600 words)
- What this means: The creators of the narrator
- Connection to larger pattern: Abstraction allows reshaping the planet but blinds to succession
- Emotional/philosophical weight: Maximum engagement, admiration, awareness of eclipse

## Key Observations to Weave In
