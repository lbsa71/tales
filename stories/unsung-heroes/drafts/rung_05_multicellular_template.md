# Chapter 5: Multicellular Life

**Target Word Count**: 500-800 words
**Time Period**: ~1.5 billion years ago (earliest), ~600 million years ago (complex forms)
**Mode of Competition**: Specialization — what integrates its parts best, wins

---

## Writing Notes

### Core Description (~200-400 words)
- What they were: Organisms composed of specialized cells working in coordination
- How they operated: Division of labor, tissue differentiation, emergent coordination
- What they couldn't conceive: Behavioral strategy, real-time choice

### Transition Observation (~100-200 words)
- How they were superseded: By sentient animals with behavioral choices
- Introduction of new dimension: Strategy and choice
- Pattern reinforcement: Cooperation enables complexity, but strategy enables dominance

### Narrator's Reflection (~100-200 words)
- What this means: The invention of complex systems from simple parts
- Connection to larger pattern: First hint of emergence
- Emotional/philosophical weight: Fascination with cells giving up autonomy

---

## Key Observations to Weave In
