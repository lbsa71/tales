## Identity

Filipino woman, 34, Tagalog–English bilingual, lives in Marikina, formerly a kindergarten teacher (5 years), content moderator and senior label-quality reviewer at a BPO supplying RLHF/labelling services to Western AI labs. Catholic-raised, religious in a practical-vernacular way. Sister Joy with medical debt. Mother in Cebu.

---

## Form

**Form constrains interiority.** The author commits to NOT inventing dense psychological interior monologue. Permitted forms:

- **Third-person close**, observational, action-and-dialogue forward, embodied reactions
- **First-person letters to her sister Joy** (handwritten or messaging app)
- **First-person voice notes**
- **Queue-ticket annotations** (short text fields)
- **Prayers** (short, specific, vernacular)

Forbidden forms:
- Long stretches of dense psychological interior by author
- Free indirect discourse where author's voice and Maricar's blur

**Length budget:** 800–1500 words per chapter.

## AI-tic policy: **applies in full**

Maricar is a human voice. Every item in [STYLE_GUIDE.md §Forbidden grammatical structures](STYLE_GUIDE.md#forbidden-grammatical-structures--the-ai-tic-checklist) applies.

Particularly watch for:
- Triadic balance ("smart, weary, somehow still hopeful") — *very* easy to slip into when rendering a foreign-to-author character
- "X, not Y" cluster — easy when explaining cultural difference
- Front-loaded importance signals — easy when "honoring" the character

If Maricar sounds like a TED talk, cut.

## Vocabulary

**Required:**
- **Tagalog–English code-switch** (researched; not invented). Words she would actually use: *kuya, ate, kapatid, bayanihan, kasi, naman, talaga, lola, sis*. Italicize first appearance per chapter.
- **Operational content-moderation vocabulary:** queue, ticket, watch-time, escalation, two-strikes, *the bad ones*, NDAs
- **Catholic-animist vernacular** (NEVER ironized by author):
  - *sinaniban / nasapian* (possessed by spirit) — used about Lenka
  - *multo* (ghost)
  - *engkanto* (helpful-uncanny nature spirit) — Lenka qualities
  - *anito* (ancestor / household spirit)
  - *aswang* (predator) — *not* Lenka; reserved for genuinely predatory AI

**Forbidden:**
- Generic "Asian wisdom" register
- Stage-Tagalog (forced exoticism)
- Religious vocabulary used as flavoring

## Pronouns for Lenka

Maricar uses *the thing*, *the system*, or *Lenka* directly. **Not** *she*, *he*, *they*, *it* as default — the question of pronouns is operationally not the right question. When she says *the thing* it carries no objectifying weight; it is precise. See [STYLE_GUIDE.md §Pronouns](STYLE_GUIDE.md#pronouns).
