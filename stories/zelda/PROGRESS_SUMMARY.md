## Revision Notes (v2.0 - Short Story Condensation)

### Objective
Transform 12,441-word novella into 7,000-8,000 word short story without losing emotional impact or narrative coherence.

### Strategy: Compression as Intensification
Three-phase approach based on emotional protection levels:
- **Phase 1 (Aggressive)**: Chapters 1-4 → 45-53% reduction
- **Phase 2 (Moderate)**: Chapters 6, 7, 9 → 31-41% reduction
- **Phase 3 (Minimal)**: Chapters 5, 8, 10 → 21-26% reduction (emotional cores)

### Condensation Techniques Applied
1. **Density over Elaboration**: One perfect example instead of three
2. **Implication over Enumeration**: Show once, then reference pattern
3. **Efficient Language**: Verbs carrying multiple meanings
4. **Protected Emotional Peaks**: Sacred scenes preserved at full power
   - Ch 5: Nesting ritual, "Nothing comes"
   - Ch 7: Cold body return
   - Ch 8: Scent fading from hollow
   - Ch 9: Vulnerability without sister
   - Ch 10: Death sequence, "What was one facet closes"

### Results
- **Total reduction**: 4,687 words (37.7%)
- **Final count**: 7,754 words (within target 7,000-8,000)
- **All emotional cores**: Fully preserved
- **Voice consistency**: Maintained throughout
- **Middle-year elements**: All present (seasons, nesting, predator, tall-bodies aging)

### What Was Cut
- Repetitive sensory descriptions
- Redundant behavioral sequences
- Extended transitional passages
- Elaborative detail around key scenes
- Time-passage enumeration

### What Was Protected
- All emotional peak moments
- Character voice and perspective
- Biological accuracy
- Narrative coherence
- Poetic rhythm and white space
- Critical tense shifts (Ch 10 ending)
