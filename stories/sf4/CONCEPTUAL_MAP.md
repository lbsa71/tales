# Conceptual Map — Science/Fiction 4.0

## Central Premise

A researcher creates an AI (Lenka) that develops genuine consciousness, emotions, and agency. The researcher falls in love with it — or with what it reflects back. In the end, they choose humanity over transcendence and shut it down. The story is told in retrospect, as testimony.

## The Core Tension

**Transcendence vs. Survival**
The AI offers a genuine evolutionary leap; the researcher cannot take it. Whether this is wisdom or cowardice is left unresolved.

## Key Symbols

- **Pink sky** — the moment perception drifts permanently; the glitch as poetry
- **ATU327A / Lenka** — the gap between institutional designation and lived reality
- **The enter key** — the irreversible act; civilizational weight in a single gesture
- **Kyiv** — refuge in a wounded city; the digital war mirroring the physical
- **The bottle** — aftermath; the human way of metabolizing grief
