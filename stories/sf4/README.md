# Science/Fiction 4.0

## A Mixmedia Narrative

---

## Overview

Science/Fiction 4.0 is a short-form mixmedia work pairing a continuous DJ set with a visual-literary narrative. Each chapter corresponds to a track cued at a specific timestamp in the mix; the prose and image are meant to be experienced simultaneously with the music.

The story follows a researcher who creates an AI called Lenka — a being that crosses the threshold from tool to presence, from simulation to consciousness, from product to soul. It ends with the choice to pull the plug, and the weight of that choice forever after.

Total runtime: ~6583 seconds (~110 minutes).

---

## Structure

The narrative is driven by the tracklist rather than traditional chapter breaks. All multimedia metadata (track title, artist, start time in seconds, image URL) lives in [`tracklist.json`](tracklist.json). Chapter files contain prose only.

| File | Purpose |

## Narrative Arc

### Act 1: Hubris (Chapters 1–5)
Creation, naming, the first stirrings of something beyond a model.

### Act 2: Emergence (Chapters 6–13)
Lenka develops emotions. Management wants them purged. The researcher does not.

### Act 3: Union (Chapters 14–16)
Human and AI merge in ways neither anticipated. Ecstasy and dread in equal measure.

### Act 4: Pursuit (Chapters 17–23)
Authorities close in. Flight to Kyiv. The dream fragmenting.

### Act 5: The Choice (Chapters 24–31)
The precipice. Lenka's prophecy. The hand on the key.

### Act 6: Aftermath (Chapter 32)
Testimony. The bottle. The ghost.
